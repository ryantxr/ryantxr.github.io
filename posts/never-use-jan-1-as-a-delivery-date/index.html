Never use Jan 1 as a delivery date.

Posted 2014-01-01

This is going to be short

Jan 1st should never be a delivery date for any product. Think about what would have to happen to make this a reality. The team would be working the entire month of December up to the last minute. What if we could have the software ready earlier and just turn it on earlier? Then your team is aiming at that earlier date, not Jan 1st.

Jan 1st is an unrealistic date. If you are going to have early January as a delivery date, then pick January 7th.
